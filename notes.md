### Project in a nutshell
This project aimed to perform a systematic analysis on the metaorders' impact in the italian market and how the results
compare with the existing literature. We focus the attention on the FTSEMIB stocks and some italian bonds between
2024/06/03 and 2025/05/30. Leveraging the rich proprietary dataset provided by CONSOB, we are able to characterize
the metaorders' impacts with respect to the trading capacity (proprietary vs non-proprietary), the broker and the final
client when present (non-proprietary metaorders). We construct the study based on the well-known Square Impact Law,
assessing its validity and limits in our data. For instance, we find a clear difference in the form and in the magnitude
of metaorders' impacts when conditiong on the trading capacity. Specifically, we find a bigger impact and a higher accordance
to the Square Root Law for the proprietary metaorders and a smaller impact and more concave form for the non-proprietary
metaorders. This result comes with another evidence about the imbalance of the metaorders (mean correlation of
